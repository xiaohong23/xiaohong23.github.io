PDZ domain of Sipa1

This is three dimensional structure of PDZ domain of Sipa1 (signal induced proliferation associated protein).

The polymorphism is located in the helical structure within PDZ domain. Ala for DBA (left panel) and Thr for FVB (right panel), represented by spacefill.

Use your left mouse button to rotate, middle mouse button to zoom in and out, and right mouse button to see more options.

The structure of DBA and FVB sipa1 was done by SwissModel.

The structure is displayed by Jmol applet.
